You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four Peanuts Gang friends below. By clicking on each peanut friend you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random number provided, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each peanut is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each peanut.
